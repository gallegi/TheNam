# Hello World

Welcome to my little corner of the internet. If you're reading this, thank you for stopping by.

I'm Nam Nguyen — an ML engineer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I've spent the last few years building machine learning systems in production: training models, debugging pipelines at 2 AM, and learning that the gap between a research notebook and a reliable production service is wider than most papers let on.

## Why a blog?

I've learned most of what I know from other people's writing. Stack Overflow answers, personal blogs, documentation written by engineers who cared enough to explain the "why" — these resources shaped how I think and work. This blog is my attempt to give something back.

I also believe that writing is thinking. When I try to explain a concept clearly, I almost always discover something I hadn't understood as well as I thought. So this blog is as much for me as it is for you.

## What you'll find here

I'll mostly write about:

- **MLOps and production ML** — how to build systems that actually work outside a Jupyter notebook
- **NLP and deep learning** — Vietnamese language models, fine-tuning, and lessons from real projects
- **Software engineering for ML** — clean code, testing, and the practices that make ML teams move faster
- **Occasional personal posts** — like this one

I won't publish on a fixed schedule. I'll write when I have something worth saying. Quality over quantity.

If anything I write is useful, wrong, or thought-provoking, I'd love to hear from you. Reach me at [namnguyen61031@gmail.com](mailto:namnguyen61031@gmail.com) or find me on GitHub.

Here's to writing more and learning in public. 🚀
